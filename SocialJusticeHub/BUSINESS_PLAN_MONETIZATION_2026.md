# Business Plan 2026-2029: Monetization Architecture for El Instante del Hombre Gris / BASTA

## 1. Executive Thesis

El Instante del Hombre Gris can become a **hybrid civic-tech + edtech + coordination platform** with three defensible revenue layers:

1. **Recurring B2C** (memberships, coaching subscriptions, learning passes).
2. **Recurring B2B/B2G** (institutional dashboards, white-label civic OS, enterprise tribe workspaces).
3. **Transactional marketplace + sales** (courses, certifications, cohort programs, events, sponsorships, impact services).

The strategic goal is to build a revenue mix where **recurring revenue reaches 60%+ by Year 3**, while transaction-based products accelerate cash generation and acquisition.

---

## 2. Why This Works Now (Market Signals)

- Argentina has high digital reach: around **41.6M internet users (89.2%) in January 2025** and **32.9M social media users (70.5%)**, enabling fast digital distribution for movement + learning products.
- World Bank data points to an Argentine population around **45.7M (2024)** and internet usage around **89.9% (2023)**, supporting national-scale digital products.
- Mercado Libre/Mercado Pago continues scaling strongly in fintech adoption, indicating a mature user behavior for online payments and wallets.
- Comparable digital education/creator ecosystems already monetize via recurring subscriptions and platform fees (Coursera Plus, Substack, Mighty Networks, Patreon, Stripe fee economics).

Implication: market readiness exists for a platform that combines civic purpose, personal transformation, and concrete coordination tools.

## 4. Revolutionary Revenue Layer (Out-of-the-Box but Viable)

These are high-upside differentiators for investor narrative and moat.

1. **Proof-of-Impact Certificates**
- Use existing blockchain certification modules to issue verifiable impact credentials for projects, cohorts, and institutions.
- Revenue: issuance fee + annual verification API subscription.

2. **Civic Intelligence API**
- Productize anonymized trend intelligence (engagement, priorities, behavioral patterns) for research institutions and NGOs.
- Revenue: tiered API subscriptions + usage overages.

3. **Outcome-Linked Contracts**
- For municipalities/foundations: paid programs where part of fee is tied to adoption or participation KPIs.
- Revenue: fixed retainer + performance bonus.

4. **National Partner Franchise Model**
- Certify local facilitators by province to run official BASTA cohorts and transformation circles.
- Revenue: annual license + rev share.

5. **Tribe Economy Toolkit**
- Add internal “credits/seeds” utility for premium governance votes, resources, and visibility boosts.
- Revenue: package sales and institutional plans funding credits for users.

## 5. Pricing Strategy (Argentina-Ready)

### 5.1 Currency and Inflation Strategy

- Anchor list prices in **USD reference tiers**.
- Charge users in ARS using a dynamic local equivalent, updated weekly.
- Offer monthly, quarterly, and annual discounts to reduce churn and inflation anxiety.
- Add local rails first (`Mercado Pago`) and global rails second (`Stripe`) for institutions/international users.

### 5.2 Recommended Initial Public Pricing

- `Free`: core map, community, limited courses.
- `Hombre Gris Plus`: USD 9/mo or USD 79/year.
- `Agente Pro`: USD 19/mo or USD 179/year.
- `Tribu Pro`: USD 49/mo base + USD 3-5 per active seat.
- `Academia Pass`: USD 14/mo or bundled in Agente Pro.
- `Institutional`: from USD 1,200/mo + onboarding.

## 12. Main Risks and Mitigation

1. **Macro volatility (Argentina)**
- Mitigation: USD-anchored pricing, annual prepayment discounts, diversified geography.

2. **Payment friction and failed renewals**
- Mitigation: multiple rails, smart retries, prepaid options, local wallet UX.

3. **High churn in consumer subscriptions**
- Mitigation: cohort-based onboarding, accountability loops, milestone rewards.

4. **Institutional long sales cycles**
- Mitigation: low-friction pilot package (8-12 weeks), clear ROI deck, modular upsell.

5. **Trust and governance concerns**
- Mitigation: transparency dashboards, moderation standards, auditable impact traces.
